What Gemma-San does

A real tutor — not a chatbot.

Gemma-San is grounded in proven teaching pedagogy: probe, build, narrow, resolve. It guides, never lectures, and adapts to each child's pace.

Socratic teaching

Asks the right question instead of giving the answer. Children learn to think, not memorise.

Talk like a human

Whisper-powered speech recognition understands children's voices and Nigerian accents naturally.

Draws & shows

22 illustrated topics plus live SVG drawing — from traffic lights to photosynthesis. Visual learning, on demand.

Remembers your child

Three-tier memory means Gemma-San remembers names, hobbies, and what was learned — across days, not just sessions.

Phonics practice

Spaced-repetition drilling adapted to each child. Five levels, mastery tracked, never overwhelming.

Quiz & celebrate

Five-question quizzes after each lesson, warm Pidgin encouragement, lesson summaries to revisit later.

How it works

Three steps. No setup pain.

Download once on Wi-Fi. After that, Gemma-San works in the village, in the danfo, on the farm — anywhere.

1

Install & download

The first launch downloads Google's Gemma 4 model (~1 GB) once. After that, no internet is ever needed again.

2

Pick an avatar, say hello

The child chooses an animal companion, picks their age, and presses the big talk button. Gemma-San greets them by name.

3

Learn through conversation

Ask a question, draw something, take a quiz, practice phonics. Gemma-San adapts to whatever the child needs.
